# Canadian Charitable Donation Tax Credit Calculator

A free, open-source tool that honestly tells Canadians what they get back from charitable donations -- including the truth about the non-refundable credit limitation that many other calculators ignore.

## Features

- Calculates federal + provincial donation tax credits
- Compares the credit to your estimated tax to show what's actually usable
- Plain-language narrative explaining your results in real numbers
- Honest about when the credit doesn't help
- No data leaves your browser -- all calculations happen client-side

## Tech stack

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
