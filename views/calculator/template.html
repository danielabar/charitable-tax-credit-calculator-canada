Your total income before tax (all sources)
What should I enter?

Enter your total yearly income before tax — from whatever sources you have. An approximate number is fine.

  • Working: your salary or wages (T4 box 14).
  • Retired: CPP, OAS, employer pension, and any RRIF withdrawals — added together.
  • Self-employed: your net business income for the year.
  • Investments: interest, dividends, and capital gains.
